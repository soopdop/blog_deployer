## At/On/In

### 기본 규칙

1. At - 하루 중 언젠가
    - at five o'clock
    - at lunchtime
    - at sunset
2. On - 일/요일
    - on Friday
    - on 16 May 1999
    - on my birthday
3. In - 달/년/계절
    - in October
    - in 1998 
    - in winter
    - in the Middle Ages
    - in the past
    - in the future

### 다음의 경우에도 at을 사용함.
 
1. at night
1. at the weekend / at weekends
1. at Christmas
1. at the moment / at present
1. at the same time 
 
### in the morning, 그러나 On Friday morning.

1. in the morning
    - on Friday morning
1. in the afternoon
    - on Sunday afternoon
1. in the evening
    - on Monday evening

### last, next, this, every 앞에는 at/on/in을 안쓴다.

1. I'll see you **next Friday.**
1. They got married **last March.**

### 얼마 후에, in

1. The train will be leaving **in a few minutes**. (몇분 후에)
1. She'll be here **in a moment**. (곧, 바로)
